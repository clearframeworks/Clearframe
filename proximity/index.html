Frequently Asked Questions

Do you change my house, driveway, or hardscape?

No. Buildings, walkways, walls, and permanent structures stay exactly as-is. We only change landscape elements like plants, beds, mulch/stone, and layout.

Is this 3D modeling?

The Photo Render Concept is photo-based visualization. Full 3D modeling is available as a separate offer.

Can I use this with my contractor?

Yes. These visuals help you communicate clearly before requesting quotes or starting installation.

Do you install the landscape?

No. Proximity provides design visuals only. Installation is handled by contractors you choose locally.
