Privacy

This site does not require you to submit sensitive information. If you contact us by email, your message will be received and used only to respond to your inquiry.

For institutional collaborations, we prefer minimal necessary information and clear boundaries. Do not email highly sensitive personal details unless specifically requested and you have appropriate consent and policies in place.

If you need a formal data handling agreement for an organizational engagement, request it via email and we will scope requirements appropriately.
