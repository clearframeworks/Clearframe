Clarity: constraint-based decision support for public systems

Clarity is a stability-first framework for mapping barriers, triaging needs, and designing steps that work under real constraints (time, access, paperwork friction, volatility, staffing, and risk).

What it doesConstraint map → option field → stabilization plan → conditional growth.
Why it helpsTargets the correct constraint instead of escalating the wrong lever.
Agency useIntake triage design, workflow friction review, eligibility/access logic.
BoundaryNot emergency services; urgent needs route to local resources first.
